How to use Agenova™

A 30-second daily habit. Consistency is what makes a supplement routine worthwhile.

Take two capsules daily

The standard serving is 2 capsules per day, together or split between morning and evening.

Pair with food & water

Take with a meal and a full glass of water. The omega-3 and fat-soluble vitamins (D & E) absorb best alongside food.

Pick a consistent time

Anchor it to an existing habit (breakfast or your evening wind-down) so you never miss a day.

Store cool & sealed

Keep the bottle tightly closed in a cool, dry place, out of reach of children.

Reorder before you run out

Questions, answered

The essentials before you start.

How many should I take and when?

Two capsules per day, ideally with a meal and water. You can take both at once or split them across the day.

How long does one bottle last?

Each bottle holds 120 capsules. At the recommended 2 per day, that's roughly 60 days, about two months.

What's the difference between the plans?

The 1-month starter is a small 60-capsule bottle (£22.99 plus £4 shipping) to try Agenova. The 2-month supply is a full 120-capsule bottle at £39.99 plus £4 shipping. The 6-month and full-year bundles ship free, and the full-year (£139.99) is the best value per capsule.

What does each capsule contain?

Vitamins C, E and D, plus zinc for skin maintenance, omega-3 fatty acids for skin health, and collagen peptides to complement a healthy lifestyle.

Can I take it with other supplements?

Agenova™ is a food supplement, not a substitute for a varied diet. If you take medication, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or have a health condition, speak to your doctor or pharmacist first.
